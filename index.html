About

WHO
I AM

Hi, I'm Rafael: a designer driven by curiosity and the excitement of building things that connect people and ideas. I've lived in places like Mexico and the United States, and those experiences shaped the way I see design: as a tool to bridge cultures, perspectives, and technology.

I work across UX, visual design, and experimentation, always looking for ways to turn complex ideas into intuitive and engaging experiences. I'm the kind of person who enjoys exploring bold ideas, collaborating with others, and pushing projects further than expected.
